DONALD E. DAVIS ARBORETUM AT AUBURN UNIVERSITY

Illustration

Logo & Branding

Visit Site

The Donald E. Davis Arboretum at Auburn University is an oasis in our community, and when they came to Here Molly Girl for a new look to carry them into the coming decades, we were thrilled. We created a comprehensive branding suite that featured the iconic Arboretum bridge and live oak leaf, as well as a fun, illustrated new visitor map and guide.  

“Auburn University’s Davis Arboretum wanted a logo that was hyper-local to our garden and also represented our core collections in a way that wasn’t too simplified or overly cumbersome. The Here Molly Girl team listened to our needs and truly delivered an iconic logo that sets us apart from other entities on campus. And the adventure map… *chef’s kiss*!”

– L. Morgan Pendergrass
Director, Davis Arboretum
